Coffee

About

Coffee, a beloved beverage enjoyed worldwide, has a rich history that can be traced back to its Latin American origins. Legend has it that it was discovered in Ethiopia and eventually made its way to Latin America through colonization and trade. Countries like Brazil, Colombia, and Guatemala have become renowned for their coffee production. Today, coffee is not just a drink; it is a social experience that can be enjoyed by everyone, regardless of their preferences or location. Coffee brings people together and delights our senses with its aroma and invigorating taste.

Ingredients

  • Coffee (preferably fresh ground)
  • Creamer (milk, half-and-half, or heavy cream)
  • Whole milk or an alternative milk such as almond, soy, or oat milk
  • Vanilla syrup or extract
  • Cinnamon powder

Instructions

  1. Brew a fresh pot or cup of coffee using your preferred brewing method.
  2. Add a desired amount of creamer to the coffee, adjusting to taste.
  3. Heat up the milk in a separate saucepan until warm.
  4. Pour the warm milk into the coffee, stirring gently to combine.
  5. Add a splash of vanilla syrup and a sprinkle of cinnamon powder to enhance flavor. Enjoy!
