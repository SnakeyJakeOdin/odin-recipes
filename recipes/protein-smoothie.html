Protein smoothie

Banana and strawberry smoothie

About

A protein smoothie is a delicious beverage made with a blend of protein sources such as protein powder, Greek yogurt, and fresh fruits. This smoothie is an excellent source of protein, providing a nutrient-dense and energy-boosting meal. Additionally, it is a great way to incorporate fruits into your daily diet, making it easy to achieve the recommended daily intake of these essential nutrients. Whether for breakfast, post-workout recovery, or a mid-day snack, a protein smoothie is a perfect way to stay nourished, energized and satisfied throughout the day.

Ingredients

  • Ice (for added thickness and texture)
  • Greek yogurt (for creaminess)
  • Milk
  • Protein powder
  • Frozen bananas
  • Frozen strawberries

Instructions

  1. Add 1 frozen banana, 1 cup of frozen strawberries, and a handful of ice to a blender.
  2. Pour in 1 cup of milk, 1/4 cut of Greek yogurt, and your desired amount of protein powder.
  3. Blend the ingredients on high speed until smooth and creamy.
  4. Taste and adjust the sweetness or texture by adding more fruit, ice, yogurt, or protein powder as desired.
